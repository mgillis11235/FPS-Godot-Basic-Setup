Who We Are

Who We Are Wooddale Middle School is a charter middle school in Memphis, TN, serving students from the Parkway Village and Hickory Hill communities. Wooddale Middle boasts a diverse student population and upholds the Green Dot Public Schools tradition of preparing students for college, leadership and life. The school serves families from a wide cross-section of racial, cultural, and socioeconomic spectrums. Since joining the Green Dot Public Schools TN family, Wooddale has made a significant impact in the community it serves, working with parents, community leaders and all stakeholders to build a great neighborhood school. Wooddale Middle enrolls grades 6 through 8.

We offer campus tours if you are interested in learning more. During the tour, attendees will have the opportunity to see the Wooddale Middle School campus, ask questions, meet the principal, and see classrooms in action.
